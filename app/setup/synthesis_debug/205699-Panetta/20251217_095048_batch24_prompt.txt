My dear Mr. Lynch:

Referring to your notice of the granting of an annuity under the Railroad Retirement Act to Illario Pannetta, Laborer, Keyser, W. May I ask, therefore, that you either send me revised form, or advise the effective date of this annuity so that I may insert the correct date in the form that I have.

Very respectfully,
