... [truncated for length]

During the handling of a distant signal switch, the lever flew up and struck him in the face due to tension in the wires, causing a fractured bone and a cut nose. The incident was investigated by Trainmaster J.J. Kahlo and attended by Dr. Mytinger. Degarmo reported the sequence of events leading to the incident, described his injuries, and confirmed he did not need to be relieved from duty on the road due to the injury.
