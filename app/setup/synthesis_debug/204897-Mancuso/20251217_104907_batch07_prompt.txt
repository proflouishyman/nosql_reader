LUIGI MANCUSO.

Baltimore, Md. March 12th, 1936.

Mr. C. C. Peery,
General Claim Agent,
Baltimore, Maryland.

Dear Sir:-

We are sending you, herewith, bill of the Yellow Cab Company of Pittsburgh, amounting to $1.65, for taxicab service on February 5th, 1936, in the case of Luigi Mancuso, Carmen, Pittsburgh Division, who was injured while on duty at Glenwood, Pennsylvania, February 5th, 1936, at which time he caught his thumb in the cradle of a car, while replacing a wheel. Very truly yours,

Medical & Surgical Director.

Enclosure.

Peery, General Claim Agent, to approve a bill of $1.65 for taxicab service related to this injury for payment processing. Mancuso is insured only for mutual death benefits.
